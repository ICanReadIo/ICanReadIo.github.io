The IcanRead app

POINT AND LISTEN

Reading should quick and effortless. For most it is, but not for everyone. Now it can be. The IcanRead app makes it effortless for everyone to read. Just point and listen.

How does it work?

IcanRead reads any text out loud, no matter if its in a book or on a screen, typed or handwriting. The voice reading the text is the most natural sounding voice available. You cannot tell it apart from a real person.

The app works on any mobile phone.

The app has two modes, handheld- and tripod-mode.

The Handheld Mode

Handheld mode is easy to get started with and is a very quick way of listening to any text. Point your phone at the text, tap and listen.

The Tripod Mode

The tripod mode is for when you want to focus even more on learning and understanding the text.

✓

Put the phone in a tripod

✓

The camera sees your book and the text in it.

✓

When you want to listen, you point at the text in the book.

✓

The app will read the text out loud for you.

The app uses advanced AI to recognize your finger and to see exactly where you point. This mode is perfect for when you study and don’t want to be distracted by your phone.

The story behind IcanRead

As a parent of a dyslexic son I help him a lot by reading for him. He quickly gets tired from reading and he has trouble taking in the subject. But the truth is that I can’t be there for him all the time. Thats why I developed IcanRead. Whenever my son is struggling with a text, he can use the app. I feel proud to give the same opportunities to learn as anyone else.

Now I’m excited to share this app with the world. I want as many families as possible to have this chance of leveling the playing field.
Adam, founder of IcanRead
